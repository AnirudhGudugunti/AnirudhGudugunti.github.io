DESCRIPTION OF CLUB

The ‘Chirppclub’ is a bird watching club.The motto of the club is to identify the habitat of different species of birds.
Member benefits include:
1)Bespoke birding tours to see the work of BirdLife Partners in the field
2)Invitations to a programme of Club events
3)Birds of Shobavana – the magazine
